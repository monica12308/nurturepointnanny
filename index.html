Services

Full-time / Part-time Nanny Care

My approach

I focus on predictable routines and calm leadership—helping kids feel secure and helping parents feel supported.

What families can expect

  • Consistent routines (meals, naps, transitions)
  • Clear communication and regular updates
  • Age-appropriate activities and structured play
  • Safety-first supervision and proactive planning
  • Respect for your household preferences and parenting style

What I do differently

  • Keep the home rhythm calm and organized
  • Model boundaries with warmth and consistency
  • Reduce chaos by planning the day in simple blocks
  • Make hand-offs easy for parents (quick recap, next steps)
  • Support long-term stability, not just “babysitting”
